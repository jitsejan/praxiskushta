About Me & The Practice

Dott.ssa Irma Kushta

Welcome to our practice network. My name is Dott.ssa Irma Kushta, specialist in internal medicine.

Born and raised in Tirana/Albania, I initially completed my human medicine studies there. In 2008, I moved with my husband and two children to beautiful Italy, where I continued my medical career.

Academic & Professional Journey

  • 2012: Final exams and state examination at the University of Bari/Italy.
  • 2013-2018: Further medical training emphasizing cardiovascular diseases at the University Polyclinic of Bari, Department of Cardiology, graduating as a specialist.
  • 2018-2022: Internist at the emergency department of SS. Annunziata Hospital (Taranto/IT).

For several years we have lived happily in beautiful Konstanz, where our third child was born in 2021.

Since July 2022 until the end of December 2022, I settled in Konstanz as an employed specialist in internal medicine (general practitioner). I would like to express my sincere thanks to my previous employer for her collegial induction to the German health care system.

It fills me with gratitude to begin the New Year 2023 with taking over the well-established practice of Mrs. S. Arens-Diem on January 2nd, 2023.

I want to care for and accompany you as a person across the entire spectrum of general and internal medicine, earn your trust, and be there for you in the proven tradition of my predecessor.

I look forward to meeting you, sincerely

Dott.ssa Irma Kushta
