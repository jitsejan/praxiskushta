Services & Specialization

As a specialist in internal medicine and a cardiologist, I offer you a comprehensive range of diagnostic and therapeutic services.

Primary Care

Comprehensive primary medical care including physical examinations, vaccinations, and preventive check-ups.

Diagnostics

  • Resting ECG
  • Exercise ECG (Ergometry)
  • Long-term blood pressure monitoring
  • Long-term ECG (Holter)
  • Lung function test (Spirometry)
  • Ultrasound (Sonography) of the abdomen and thyroid

Cardiology

Diagnostic evaluation, therapy, and consultation for cardiovascular diseases such as hypertension, heart failure, and coronary artery disease.

Laboratory Diagnostics

Comprehensive laboratory tests including blood, urine, and stool analysis for accurate diagnosis and monitoring.

Disease Management Programs (DMP)

Structured treatment programs for chronic diseases to improve care and quality of life.

Geriatric Assessments

Specialized examinations and care plans tailored to the needs of elderly patients.
